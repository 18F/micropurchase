Placing a bid

18F’s Micro-purchase Platform requires that all vendors register with SAM.gov and provide a valid DUNS number before placing a bid. This process normally takes about two weeks and involves the following steps:

  1. Register on SAM.gov
  2. Request a DUNS number
  3. Update your profile.
