Placing a bid

Before you can bid on an auction, you’ll need to register on SAM.gov, request (or locate) a DUNS number, and then update your SAM.gov profile to include your DUNS number. See our guide for more information

Getting paid

Before we can paid you, we’ll need a location we can visit to issue payment. This is your payment URL. If you’re unfamiliar with payment processing, there are a number of different ways to do this. See our guide for more information.
