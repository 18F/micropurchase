Placing a bid

Before you can bid on an auction, you’ll need to register on SAM.gov, request a DUNS number, and link your SAM.gov profile with your DUNS number. See our guide for more information.

Getting paid

Before 18F can pay you, we’ll need a location we can visit to issue payment. You can either specify a default payment URL or provide this to us whenever you win an auction. See our guide for more information.
