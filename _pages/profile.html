Placing a bid

Before you can bid on an auction, you’ll need to register your business with SAM.gov, request (or locate) a DUNS number, and then update your SAM.gov profile to include your DUNS number.

Getting paid

Before you can get paid you’ll need to setup a payment URL. There are a number of different ways to do this. For example, you might use Stripe or Square Cash. See our guide for more information.
