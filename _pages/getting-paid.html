Getting paid

18F’s Micro-purchase Platform gives vendors the flexibility to choose the payment processor that works best for them. There are a number of options are available — including Square Cash and Stripe — but all we really need is a place to enter our credit card information! This is your Payment URL.

To specify or update your Payment URL, simply visit your profile. We’ll issue payment against it whenever you win an auction and meet its acceptance criteria before the delivery deadline.

PS: One of our community members wrote a handy app to deploy a Stripe payment form via Heroku. Also, please let us know if you’re interested in using PayPal to accept payments.
